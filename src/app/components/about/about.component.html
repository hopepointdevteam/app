About Us

A Church for the broken

Hope Point is a church for people of all walks of life. Imagine a community with one focus; bringing our city closer to God. This is what Hope Point stands for.
We are a multi-generatoinal, multi-ethnic, multi-cultural church located in Yuba City, California. Our church has grown to be represented in Live Oak, Sacramento, the Bahamas, and globaly through our missionaries.

That loves jesus

Jesus promises an abundant, overflowing, changing life. It is not a life free from sorrow or pain, but equiped to handle the everyday situations we encounter.
Hope Point wants to praise Jesus and those who choose to follow and fall in love with Him.
