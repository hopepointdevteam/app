About

Hope Point Ministries for adults age 55 is as diverse as the Builders and Boomers that make up our age group. Born in the early 1900s to 1960s, we bond together in blessings and challenges unique to Life After Fifty-Five (L.A.F.F.). Please check the calendar for weekly, monthly, and periodic events that include the PT Cruisers Potluck Fellowship with guest speaker (3rd Sunday each month), Sunday school, prayer walks and a variety of activities including bicycling, campouts, and outings.
